# Artificial Intelligence in Daily Life – Educational Infographic Design

## Overview

This project showcases an educational infographic titled **"Artificial Intelligence in Daily Life"**, designed to explain how AI is transforming modern society. The infographic presents AI concepts, applications, benefits, challenges, and future impact through a visually engaging and easy-to-understand layout.

## Design Concept

The infographic is designed to communicate complex AI concepts in a simple and visually appealing manner while maintaining a futuristic technology-inspired aesthetic.

The design focuses on:

* AI awareness and education
* Real-world applications of AI
* Benefits and challenges of AI technologies
* Easy-to-understand information hierarchy
* Modern and engaging visual presentation

## Design Elements

### AI-Themed Illustrations

Futuristic robot and AI-inspired graphics are used to visually represent artificial intelligence and technological innovation.

### Information Sections

The infographic is divided into multiple sections covering:

* Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
* AI applications in everyday life
* Benefits of AI
* Challenges and concerns
* Conclusion and future outlook

### Color Palette

* Purple & Blue Neon: Innovation, technology, and intelligence
* Black Background: Modern, futuristic, and professional appearance
* White Typography: High readability and contrast

### Typography

Bold and modern typography is used to create a clear visual hierarchy and improve content accessibility.
